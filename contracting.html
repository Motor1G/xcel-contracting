What We Offer

Our Contracting Services

We manage every phase of your residential remodel — from first swing of the demo hammer to final coat of paint.

🔩

Demolition

Safe, efficient, and clean demo work. We remove walls, flooring, cabinets, fixtures, and more — with full debris hauling and site prep for the next phase.

🏗️

Framing

Structural and non-structural framing for room additions, open-concept conversions, accent walls, and entertainment wall build-outs.

🪵

Flooring

Hardwood, LVP, tile, carpet, and laminate installation. We prep the subfloor, install with precision, and finish with clean transitions and trim.

🧱

Drywall & Texture

Full drywall hang, tape, mud, and texture. From smooth walls to knockdown and orange peel — we match your existing texture seamlessly.

🎨

Interior Painting

Professional interior painting with proper prep, clean lines, and long-lasting results. Walls, ceilings, trim, cabinets, and accent walls.

🔧

Trim & Finish Work

Baseboards, crown molding, door casing, window trim, wainscoting, and custom millwork. The details that make a room feel finished.

🌡️

HVAC Coordination

We work with licensed HVAC partners for duct relocation, register moves, and system additions as part of your remodel scope.

💡

Electrical & Lighting

Coordination of licensed electricians for outlet relocation, recessed lighting, accent lighting, and panel work as needed for your project.

🚿

Bathroom Remodels

Full bath and powder room remodels — tile showers, vanity replacement, flooring, lighting, and fixtures. We manage the whole scope.

🍳

Kitchen Updates

Backsplash tile, cabinet painting or refacing, countertop coordination, flooring, and trim updates. Kitchen refreshes done right.
